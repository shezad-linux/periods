📋 1. Overview

CycleCare is a local-first, offline menstrual cycle and wellness tracking application for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it stays on your device.

Effective Date: 23 March 2026

📝 2. Information We Collect

CycleCare does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on remote servers. All data you enter — including cycle dates, symptoms, mood, and notes — is stored exclusively on your device.

  • No account or sign-up required — the app works immediately after install.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs — we do not embed third-party analytics.
  • No advertising identifiers — the app contains no ads.

💾 3. How Your Data Is Stored

All health and cycle data is persisted in a local database on your device. Data is never uploaded to the cloud unless you explicitly choose to export it.

  • Data remains on your device and under your control at all times.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data.
  • Optional data export produces files that are saved to your device's local storage.

🔗 4. Data Sharing

CycleCare does not share, sell, or transfer your personal data to any third parties. Because no data leaves your device, there are no external recipients, advertising networks, or data brokers involved.

🔑 5. App Permissions

CycleCare requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function:

  • Notifications — Used to send optional cycle and wellness reminders that you configure. Notification content is designed to be discreet; no sensitive health details are shown on the lock screen.
  • Boot Completed — Used to reschedule your reminders after a device restart so you never miss a notification you set up.
CycleCare does not request Internet, Camera, Location, Contacts, or any other sensitive permissions.

🛡️ 6. Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • PIN Lock — An optional in-app PIN lock prevents unauthorised access to the app.
  • Hidden Notification Previews — Sensitive information is hidden from lock-screen notification previews.
  • No Network Communication — Since the app operates entirely offline, your data cannot be intercepted in transit.

👶 7. Children's Privacy

CycleCare is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because all data is stored locally and never transmitted, the app does not process children's data on any server.

⚕️ 8. Health Disclaimer

CycleCare provides estimates and trend summaries for personal wellness tracking only. The app is not intended to be used for:

  • Medical diagnosis or treatment decisions
  • Contraception planning or fertility advice
  • Emergency health guidance
Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice. CycleCare is a wellness tool, not a medical device.

🔄 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
